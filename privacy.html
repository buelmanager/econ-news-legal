ECON NEWS — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how "ECON NEWS" ("the Service") handles data. The Service is a personal automation tool that posts the operator's own videos to the operator's own TikTok account. It is not affiliated with TikTok.

2. Data We Access

  • TikTok authorization token: When the operator connects their TikTok account, the Service receives an OAuth access token used solely to upload and publish videos to that account via TikTok's Content Posting API. Scopes used: user.info.basic (basic profile), video.upload / video.publish (posting).
  • Video content: The short-form videos the operator creates and chooses to publish.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Service does not collect personal information from TikTok viewers or any third parties. It does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

4. Storage & Security

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform the operator's requested posting actions. The operator can revoke access at any time from their TikTok account settings.

5. Retention & Deletion

Tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the Service and are deleted upon disconnection.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated; the "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
